About Me

I was born in Kasaragod district in northern Kerala where the district itself has many different languages and traditions. It helped me explore myself and choose my career path at a very early stage. I wanted to become an IT professional and as a first step, I chose computer science right after class 10th, where I learned basic programming skills. I was more focused on what I wanted to be, so I chose BCA as my next step, followed by MCA.After graduation I found a job in a startup company as a Java mobile application developer.

     We humans are passionate about the things we love the most. Every time I get a new project, I try to reduce complexity and make it easier for the user to interact with the product. As a result, it helps me become more proficient in programming and improve my project management skills.

    I loved Java programming, so I kept it forever. As a developer, I get many opportunities to interact with clients, which makes my job easier. As soon as I understood their needs, I developed creative solutions. Gradually, I transformed to a full-stack developer.
